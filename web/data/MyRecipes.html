Amazing Meat Loaf (serves 4)

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 lb. ground pork
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup dry quick oats
  • 1/2 envelope dry onion soup mix

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix 3 Tbsp ketchup, beef, pork, eggs, oats and soup mix. Shape into a loaf. Place on a greased cookie sheet. Top with 1 Tbsp ketchup. Bake until meat thermometer inserted in the center reads 160 degrees F, 1 to 1 1/4 hours. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.

BBQ Chicken Quesadillas

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 cup canned red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels, defrosted and drained if frozen
  • 1 tbsp chopped pickled jalapeño
  • 1 cup slow cooker bbq chicken
  • 8 taco-size whole-wheat tortillas
  • 8 tbsp shredded reduced-fat cheddar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°F. Warm skillet over medium heat; mist with cooking spray. Sauté onion until tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Add spinach and cook, stirring, until wilted. Season with salt and pepper and remove from heat.
  2. In a bowl, mash beans with a fork. Add spinach mixture, corn, jalapeño and chicken. Spread 1/2 cup of filling on half of a tortilla, sprinkle with 1 tbsp cheddar and fold tortilla over. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
  3. Warm a large skillet over medium heat. Cook quesadillas in a single layer until brown and heated through, about 4 minutes per side. Place on a baking sheet in oven to keep warm; cook remaining quesadillas. Cut each into thirds; serve 4 wedges per person.

Chili Con Carne (Serves 4)

Description:

From Joy of Cooking

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup store-bought chili powder
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 -1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onions, minced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3-4 fresh jalapeno peppers, stemmed and minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 15-ounce can plum tomatoes, with juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 3 cups water
  • 1-2 cans beans
  • shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions:

  1. Season meat with salt. Brown in 1 Tbsp olive oil and then remove to Dutch oven or large stock pot.
  2. Cook onions, garlic and jalapenos in 1 Tbsp olive oil, stirring often, over medium-high heat until the vegetables are softened, 6-8 minutes. Remove to the Dutch oven with the meat. Stir the chili powder into the meat mixture and cook for 2 minutes over medium-high heat.
  3. Add tomatoes, vinegar and water. Simmer, uncovered, until the meat is tender and the sauce is reduced and thickened, about 1 1/2 hours. Stir in beans 30 minutes before end time, heating through.
  4. Serve with sour cream, shredded cheese and crackers.

Mom's Rigatoni Sauce

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb. stewing beef
  • 1/2 lb. pork cubes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • olive oil
  • 1 large can Hunt's Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 large can Hunt's Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 small can Contadina tomato paste
  • 1/2 packet Italian seasoning
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • oregano
  • basil
  • salt & pepper
  • 1-2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 16 ounces rigatoni noodles

Instructions:

  1. 1. Brown/sear meat in oil at medium-high heat. Season with salt, pepper and garlic.
  2. 2. Pour in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, Italian seasoning, and bay leaves. Add enough oregano and basil to cover top of sauce. Mix, then cover and simmer all day.
  3. 3. Cook noodles according to package. Drain and place in long dish.
  4. 4. Drizzle noodles with sauce and mozzarella. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes, until cheese is melted.

Saucy Turkey Breast

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 3-5-lb. turkey breast, bone-in or boneless
  • 1 envelope dry onion soup mix
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 16-oz. can cranberry sauce, jellied or whole-berry
  • 2 Tbsp. cornstarch
  • 2 Tbsp. cold water

Instructions:

  1. 1. Sprinkle salt and pepper and soup mix on the top and bottom of turkey. Place turkey in slow cooker.
  2. 2. Add cranberry sauce to top of turkey breast.
  3. 3. Cover and cook on Low 4-5 hours on High 1-3 hours, or until tender. A meat thermometer should read 180 degrees.
  4. 4. Remove turkey from cooker and allow to rest for 10 minutes. Keep sauce in cooker.
  5. 5. Cover cooker and tun to High. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch and water. When sauce is boiling, add mixture and simmer until sauce thickens.
  6. 6. Slice turkey and serve with sauce.
